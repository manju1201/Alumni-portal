Conditions of Participation

Can we donate clothes/other items ?

Well you can donate and items you'd like to. Mention them in the description, and we'll contact you.

What is your organization’s mission ?

Our mission is raise $1 Million.

Whoops! I didn’t mean to donate that amount ?

So you made a wrong donation, no problem. Contact us through our contact form and we'll get back to you.

How can I donate to honor or remember someone ?

You can mention his/her name in the description.

What do you do with my donor information ?

We save your name and conctact information so if needed we can can contact you later.

Do I have to pay a tax ?

No you are not paying any tax through this donation. 100% of your donation is going for cause.

How can I make an in-kind donation ?

We have provided a designation dropdown in our form. Through that you can donate to a specific cause.

What routes should one follow?

Go to the top of this page and press the "Donate Now" link, fill the form and yay you have donated.

What percentage of my donation goes to the cause ?

Whatever cause you mention in your designation, we are forwarding 100% of it to the designated cause.
